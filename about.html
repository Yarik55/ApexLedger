Our approach

Software where it's fast.
A professional where it matters.

Budgeting apps show you numbers. Accountants lodge your tax. ApexLedger connects the two — the money you track every day flows straight through to the professional preparing your return.

Philosophy

Your numbers shouldn't live in two worlds.

Most people track spending in one app, keep receipts in a drawer, and hand their accountant a shoebox in July. Every hand-off loses detail, and every gap costs deductions.

ApexLedger closes the gaps. Record a transaction on your phone and it's already on your web dashboard, already categorised for tax, already visible to your accountant — with the receipt attached. When a question comes up, you chat in the portal instead of digging through email.

  • One source of truth — app, web and accountant see the same data
  • Tax thinking built in from the first transaction
  • Live chat instead of email chains
  • Bank-grade security with row-level data isolation
